Oranje Commercial Janitorial is a certified women owned business founded in 2005 by Ryan and Maggie Stark. Both graduates of Boston College, they weren’t afraid to get dirty; working hand-on, 7 days per week, 365 days per year. Pouring their heart and soul into the business, they were able to find success, growing from literally zero employees and customers, to be recognized as Arizona’s leading janitorial service provider with over over 400 customers and nearing 1,000 janitorial associates. Despite the company’s success, they pride themselves as being large enough to execute, but still small enough to care. You can pickup the phone and reason their CEO Joshua Woodworth anytime, for any reason.
